		IF_PASSED true positive (good)
	</p>
{+END}

	<p>
		IF_PASSED false positive (bad)
	</p>
{+END}
{+START,IF_NON_PASSED,PASSED}
	<p>
		IF_PASSED false negative (bad)
	</p>
{+END}
{+START,IF_NON_PASSED,PASSED_FAKE}
	<p>
		IF_PASSED true negative (good)
	</p>
{+END}
